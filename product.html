Who we are

At Zoomies, we strongly believe that both dogs and their owners should positively complement each other in their relationship.

Although walking your dog is one of the most important activities in this relationship, since it improves the dog’s health and social behavior, it is often viewed as a “mandatory task”.

We advocate for changing this view by positively impacting the dog walking experience through maximizing community, joy, freedom, and responsibility for every dog owner.

Our vision

In a world where owning a dog can occasionally feel solitary, we believe that unplanned interactions amongst like-minded dog owners will build stronger networks and more satisfying experiences. Only because then, every park stroll, every trip together, and every tail wag will turn into a chance for bonding for dogs and even their owners.
