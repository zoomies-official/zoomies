OUR PRODUCT

We’re excited to introduce the Zoomies App! A new way to connect, explore, and enjoy life with your dog. At the heart of our app is an interactive map designed to support dog owners in their daily routines and enhance their dog walking experiences. The app fosters the creation of small, local communities of dog owners in every neighborhood, helping people build connections right where they live. Through messaging features, dog owners can easily reach out to fellow walkers nearby, supporting our core value of community. Beyond connection, the Zoomies App helps users balance work, life, and social well-being. By encouraging social interactions through shared dog walking experiences, it promotes the values of joy and freedom, creating new opportunities for owners to unwind, meet people, and strengthen their bond with their dogs. Finally, our interactive map highlights safe walking routes and dog-friendly locations, addressing a common challenge for dog owners: finding safe, welcoming spaces to walk and explore. With Zoomies, every walk is more than just a routine, it’s a chance to connect, explore, and belong.

Our vision

In a world where owning a dog can occasionally feel solitary, we believe that unplanned interactions amongst like-minded dog owners will build stronger networks and more satisfying experiences. Only because then, every park stroll, every trip together, and every tail wag will turn into a chance for bonding for dogs and even their owners.
