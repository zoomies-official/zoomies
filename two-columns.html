About Zoomies

Who We Are

Zoomies is a brand that is designed to promote the most loveable relationship between dog-owners and their dogs. We are a strong believer in the expression ‚ÄúHappy dog, happy owner & happy owner, happy dog‚Äù because we believe that the dogs and their owners should positively complement each other. Zoomies is a big advocate for pet well-being, but we also value the joy, freedom, and responsibility of being a dog owner. Besides this, we find it important that a community can be felt amongst dog-owners since they are the only people who truly understand the struggles and benefits of having a dog.

Our Vision

In a world where owning a dog can occasionally feel solitary, we believe that unplanned interactions amongst like-minded dog owners will build stronger networks and more satisfying experiences. Only because then, every park stroll, every trip together, and every tail wag will turn into a chance for bonding for dogs and even their owners.
